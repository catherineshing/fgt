Established in 2009, Fruitful Green Tech is a professional manufacturer and exporter that is concerned with the design, development, and production of 3D EVA shower curtains, table cloths, and EVA film. We are located in Shenzhen, Guangdong province, with convenient transportation access. All of our products comply with international quality standards and are greatly appreciated in a variety of different markets throughout the world.

Covering an area of 4500 square matters, we now have over 30 employees, boast an annual sales figure that exceeds $5 million USD and currently export 50% of our production worldwide.

As a result of our high quality products and outstanding custom service, we have gained a global sales network reaching European countries.
